How to Use

  1. Select your position: Long or Short.
  2. Enter your Risk-Reward Ratio (RR).
  3. Enter your Entry Price.
  4. Choose whether you want to enter Stop Loss or Target Price.
  5. Input one of them, and click Calculate.
  6. The tool will show you the missing value instantly.

Tip: You can install this as a mobile app via PWA for quick access!
