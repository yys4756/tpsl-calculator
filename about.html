📌 About This Calculator

✅ A super-fast, mobile-friendly tool for traders. Just input your entry price and RR (Risk Reward Ratio), and instantly get your stop loss or target price.
🎯 Made for speed. No login, no ads, no data stored.

🛠️ How to Use

  • Choose your position: Long or Short
  • Enter your entry price and RR (Risk/Reward)
  • Choose what you want to calculate: Stop Loss or Target Price
  • Input one value (either stop or target) and click Calculate
  • 🎉 Result will appear instantly

📄 Terms of Use

This tool is provided as-is. It is meant to assist in trading calculations, not replace professional judgment. Use at your own discretion.

🔒 Privacy

We store nothing. All calculations are done 100% in your browser. No data leaves your device. No cookies. No tracking.
